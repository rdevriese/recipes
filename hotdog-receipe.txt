Ingredients:
 - 1 nice hot dog
 - 1 hot dog bun
 - chopped onions
 - mustard
 - Catsup
 
Directions:
 1. Grill hot dog on charcoal grill
 2. Toast inside of hotdog bun until nicely brown
 3. Place hotdog inside of bun
 4. Add chopped onions, mustard, and catsup to your liking
 5. Eat and enjoy!
 6. Wash down with a nice micro brew of your choice.
